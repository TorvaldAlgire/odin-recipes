Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Chicken vegetable stir fry is a quick and flavorful meal that combines tender chicken pieces with crisp vegetables in a savory sauce. The dish is cooked in a hot pan or wok, which allows the ingredients to cook rapidly while maintaining their texture and color. A simple sauce made from soy sauce, garlic, and ginger coats the chicken and vegetables, creating a balanced dish that is both healthy and satisfying. It is commonly served over rice or noodles.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced thin
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  • ¼ cup water
  • Cooked rice for serving

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a small bowl mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, and water to create the stir fry sauce. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat.
  3. Add the sliced chicken and cook until lightly browned and nearly cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  4. In the same pan add garlic and ginger and cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add broccoli, carrots, and bell pepper and stir fry for about 3 to 4 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  6. Return the chicken to the pan and stir everything together.
  7. Pour the prepared sauce into the pan and stir continuously until it thickens and coats the ingredients.
  8. Cook for another 1 to 2 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  9. Serve hot over rice.
